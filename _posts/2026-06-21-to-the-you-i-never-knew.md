title: to the you i never knew
---

there is more to say,
yet maybe words carry meaning not enough

i'd say how i'm grateful for your presence
and how worth your hard work was to raise us

...then what next?

there must be more to say,
yet i forget, knowing not who you were anymore
not your face, your voice, nor the you from before

was this the life you had envisioned for me?
how shall i know, if your ashes here
come from slowly fading pages that i cannot see

broken concrete and roots overgrown
when time took you away from here
my wish changed naught no matter how sincere
how so futile, the dandelion seeds i've blown

with time came the words "should-have-been"
and every future memory to be made
slowly faded into the "if-only"s i'd prayed

there should've been more to say
yet i know this will never reach you,
to the you i never knew

happy father's day
